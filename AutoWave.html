Auto Wave

With Dr. Trevor Bihl, Dr. Aaron Jones, Peter John-Baptiste, Ashley Demange, Patrick Farr
and many more

Auto Wave is an AFRL project seeking to use deep learning and hardware acceleration to revolutionize waveform design. We use neural networks and neuromorphic hardware to accelerate cognitive radio techniques to near-real time.

The implications of Auto Wave touch all RF applications, from UAV and VANETs to communication systems. Below are all materials about the project available for public release, including papers and conference presentations.
